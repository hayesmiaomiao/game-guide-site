## Quick Answer

Ranni's questline is one of Elden Ring's most important side stories because it unlocks major areas, connects several NPCs, and leads to the Age of Stars ending. Start by meeting Ranni at Ranni's Rise in Liurnia, speak with her companions, defeat Radahn to open the path to Nokron, collect the Fingerslayer Blade, then follow the Ainsel River and Lake of Rot route toward the Moonlight Altar. The quest is long, but it is manageable if you treat it as a chain of area unlocks rather than one continuous checklist.

## Introduction

Ranni's questline sends you across Liurnia, Caelid, Nokron, Ainsel River, Lake of Rot, and the Moonlight Altar. It is easy to lose the thread because Elden Ring rarely marks the next step directly. This guide keeps the route in order, explains what each step unlocks, and highlights the points where players most often think the quest is broken.

## Detailed Guide

### Starting Ranni's Quest

After reaching Liurnia, travel to Caria Manor and clear the path to the Three Sisters area. Ranni waits in Ranni's Rise after the manor boss. Speak with her and agree to serve her. Then talk to the spectral forms of Blaidd, Seluvis, and Iji in the tower before speaking to Ranni again. This step matters because leaving early can make the next objective feel unclear. If Ranni is missing, check whether festival conditions or other quest states have shifted; in most cases, progressing Radahn or returning later resolves the issue.

### Radahn and Nokron

The quest points you toward Nokron, but the city cannot be properly accessed until Starscourge Radahn is defeated in Caelid. After Radahn falls, a star opens a crater in Limgrave. Descend carefully into Nokron and work through the city until you obtain the Fingerslayer Blade from the Night's Sacred Ground route. Bring the blade back to Ranni. This is the point where the quest changes from political mystery to cosmic journey, and it also unlocks further access tied to Renna's Rise.

### Ainsel River and the Miniature Ranni

Use the portal in Renna's Rise to reach Ainsel River Main and pick up the Miniature Ranni doll nearby. Rest at a Site of Grace and choose the option to speak with the doll multiple times. Many players miss this because the first attempts seem quiet. Keep trying until Ranni responds. Continue through Ainsel River and Nokstella while watching for strong enemies and upgrade materials. The route eventually leads toward the Baleful Shadow encounter, which advances the quest and gives the key needed for a later chest.

### Lake of Rot and Moonlight Altar

After the Baleful Shadow, continue toward the Lake of Rot. Use Preserving Boluses, immunity gear, or careful platform routing to reduce rot buildup. Cross the lake, proceed through the Grand Cloister, and defeat Astel, Naturalborn of the Void. With the correct key and ring step completed, you can access the Moonlight Altar above Liurnia. Finish the final interaction below the Cathedral of Manus Celes to complete Ranni's story and unlock the Age of Stars ending option.

## Tips

- Talk to every Ranni tower NPC before leaving the first meeting.
- Defeat Radahn if Nokron progression seems blocked.
- Speak to Miniature Ranni multiple times at the grace.
- Bring rot protection before crossing Lake of Rot.

## Common Mistakes

- Expecting quest markers after every step.
- Missing the Fingerslayer Blade route in Nokron.
- Forgetting to open the relevant chest after the Baleful Shadow.
- Entering Lake of Rot without boluses or enough flasks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I fail Ranni's questline?

It is difficult to permanently fail, but certain steps can appear stalled until you advance Radahn, tower dialogue, or the doll interaction.

### Where does Ranni go after Nokron?

After receiving the Fingerslayer Blade, Ranni leaves and the next route opens through Renna's Rise.

### Why won't Miniature Ranni talk?

Rest at the correct Site of Grace and choose the talk option several times. She does not respond immediately.

### Does Ranni's quest unlock an ending?

Yes. Completing it unlocks the Age of Stars ending option after the final boss.

### Is Ranni's quest worth doing?

Yes. It unlocks major areas, important lore, valuable rewards, and one of Elden Ring's most popular endings.
