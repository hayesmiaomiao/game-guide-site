## Quick Start Priorities

Your first goal is not to clear every enemy. It is to unlock Sites of Grace, collect map fragments, upgrade your main weapon, and build enough Flask charges to survive mistakes.

Focus on these early priorities:

- Unlock Church of Elleh and Gatefront Ruins.
- Get Torrent as soon as Melina appears.
- Pick one main weapon and upgrade it instead of spreading materials.
- Put early levels into Vigor before chasing damage stats.
- Use guard counters if you start with a shield.

## First 10 Hours Route

Start in Limgrave and move carefully through low-risk camps. Gatefront Ruins is a strong practice area because enemies are spaced out and the Site of Grace is close.

After you unlock Torrent, ride south toward the Weeping Peninsula. This area is more forgiving than Stormveil Castle and gives you extra upgrade materials, Sacred Tears, and Golden Seeds.

## Early Stat Advice

For most beginners, Vigor should reach 20 before damage stats become a priority. Surviving one extra hit matters more than adding a few points of weapon scaling.

If you are unsure what to level:

1. Raise Vigor.
2. Meet minimum weapon requirements.
3. Add Endurance if your gear load is too heavy.
4. Improve Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Faith, or Arcane only after your build direction is clear.

## Boss Preparation

Before fighting Margit, aim for a +3 weapon, extra Flask charges, and a Spirit Ash summon you understand. Margit punishes panic rolls, so watch the delayed attacks and roll after the swing begins.

## Common Beginner Mistakes

Do not sell unique weapons, ignore crafting recipes, or assume every visible boss must be defeated immediately. Elden Ring rewards leaving hard fights, finding power elsewhere, and coming back prepared.
