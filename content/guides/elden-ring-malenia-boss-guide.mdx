## Quick Answer

Malenia is difficult because every hit she lands heals her, so the fight is less about racing damage and more about denying free contact. Use a fully upgraded weapon, high Vigor, reliable bleed or frost if your build supports it, and a plan for Waterfowl Dance before you enter. Keep medium distance, punish only clear recoveries, and treat phase two as a reset rather than a victory lap. If you can survive Waterfowl, avoid greedy trades, and keep calm after Scarlet Aeonia, Malenia becomes learnable instead of impossible.

## Introduction

Malenia, Blade of Miquella is Elden Ring's most famous optional boss for a reason. She combines fast sword pressure, lifesteal, scarlet rot, and one of the most punishing signature moves in the game. This guide focuses on repeatable decisions: spacing, safe punish windows, summon strategy, and the habits that keep her from healing back all your progress.

## Detailed Guide

### Preparation and Build Priorities

Bring a maxed or near-maxed weapon and enough Vigor to survive a partial combo. Malenia punishes low health harder than almost any boss because even blocked or mistimed exchanges can refill her health. Bleed and frost are strong because they reward consistent pressure, but pure strength, dexterity, faith, and intelligence builds can all win if they avoid trades. Light load can help with spacing, but medium load is perfectly viable. Choose talismans that improve survivability, stamina, damage consistency, or your main attack pattern rather than glass-cannon bonuses that encourage risky trades.

### How to Survive Waterfowl Dance

Waterfowl Dance is the move that decides most attempts. The safest answer is prevention through spacing: do not stand directly under Malenia when she is likely to use it, especially around mid health. If she rises while you are far away, sprint backward from the first flurry, dodge through or away from the second, then roll through the final tracking burst. If she starts close, use a shield, Bloodhound Step, Freezing Pots, or practiced close-range dodge timing depending on your build. The key is having one chosen answer before the fight starts. Improvising after she jumps usually ends badly.

### Phase One Strategy

Phase one is about discipline. Many of her sword strings end with a visible pause, but she can also cancel pressure in ways that punish automatic counterattacks. Circle at medium range, let her finish, then land a single strong punish. Jump attacks, running attacks, and quick weapon arts can work well, but stop attacking as soon as she regains control. If you use a summon, avoid surrounding her in a way that makes her movement unpredictable. Stay ready to disengage when her posture changes or she backs away.

### Phase Two Strategy

Phase two begins with Scarlet Aeonia. Roll away from the dive, wait for the bloom, then punish with ranged damage or rebuff while the flower fades. The second phase is more aggressive and adds rot pressure, but her healing rule remains the central problem. Do not chase into lingering rot, and do not trade just because she has low health. Save enough flasks for the final third of the fight, where nerves cause most mistakes. A clean final minute beats a desperate damage race.

## Tips

- Enter with a specific Waterfowl answer.
- Favor safe single-hit punishes over long combos.
- Use frost or bleed if your build can apply it consistently.
- Do not chase her through rot clouds in phase two.
- Reset mentally after Scarlet Aeonia; the fight is only halfway done.

## Common Mistakes

- Trying to trade because she appears low on health.
- Learning Waterfowl only after reaching the fight.
- Using a summon that lets her heal constantly without coordinated pressure.
- Standing too close when she pauses at mid range.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Malenia required to beat Elden Ring?

No. Malenia is optional, but she is one of the most valuable late-game challenge fights for experienced players.

### What is Malenia weak to?

Bleed and frost are commonly effective, but execution and avoiding lifesteal matter more than any single weakness.

### Can shields work against Malenia?

A shield can help survive Waterfowl, but blocked hits may still let her heal, so it should be a backup rather than the entire plan.

### Should I use Mimic Tear for Malenia?

Mimic Tear can help if your setup applies strong pressure, but it can also give her healing opportunities if it gets hit repeatedly.

### How do I punish Scarlet Aeonia?

Dodge the dive, wait outside the bloom, then use ranged attacks, buffs, or a safe approach as the flower disappears.
