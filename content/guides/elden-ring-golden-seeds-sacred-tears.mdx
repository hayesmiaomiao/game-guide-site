## Quick Answer

Golden Seeds and Sacred Tears are two of the most important upgrades in Elden Ring. Golden Seeds increase the number of flask charges, while Sacred Tears increase how much each flask restores. New players should prioritize both before difficult bosses because extra healing often matters more than a few levels. Look for Golden Seeds near small golden saplings and Sacred Tears inside churches. Build your early route around Limgrave, Weeping Peninsula, and Liurnia churches and saplings to make the rest of the game much more forgiving.

## Introduction

If Elden Ring feels brutally punishing, your flask may be under-upgraded. Golden Seeds and Sacred Tears quietly determine how many mistakes you can survive, how far you can explore, and how confidently you can learn boss patterns. This guide explains what to collect first and how to think about flask upgrades.

## Detailed Guide

### Golden Seeds vs Sacred Tears

Golden Seeds add flask charges. Sacred Tears increase flask strength. Both are valuable, but they solve slightly different problems. More charges help long exploration routes and repeated chip damage. Stronger flasks help when bosses hit hard and you need one heal to recover most of your health. A balanced upgrade path is best: do not ignore churches while chasing seeds, and do not skip saplings because your flasks already heal a lot.

### Where to Find Golden Seeds

Golden Seeds are commonly found near glowing golden saplings along roads, near major landmarks, and sometimes after specific encounters. When entering a new region, scan roads and open areas for the small golden tree. Many seeds can be collected without clearing a dungeon, which makes them excellent early power gains. If you are struggling with a main boss, exploring for seeds is usually more efficient than grinding levels.

### Where to Find Sacred Tears

Sacred Tears are usually inside churches. Once you reveal a map fragment, look for church-like structures and route to them safely. The Weeping Peninsula is especially useful early because it offers low-pressure exploration and flask upgrades. Churches may also contain Sites of Grace, NPCs, or other useful items, making them strong route anchors for beginners.

### How to Prioritize Flask Allocation

After upgrading, decide how many Crimson and Cerulean flasks your build needs. Pure melee builds usually want more Crimson flasks, while casters need enough Cerulean to maintain spell pressure. Hybrid builds should adjust by activity: more FP for exploration or magic-heavy bosses, more healing when learning a dangerous melee fight. Reallocate flasks at Sites of Grace whenever your plan changes.

## Tips

- Search golden saplings for Golden Seeds.
- Check churches for Sacred Tears.
- Upgrade flasks before forcing a hard boss.
- Reallocate Crimson and Cerulean charges by build.

## Common Mistakes

- Grinding levels while ignoring flask upgrades.
- Collecting map fragments but not checking churches.
- Keeping too many FP flasks on a melee build.
- Saving upgrades instead of using them immediately.
