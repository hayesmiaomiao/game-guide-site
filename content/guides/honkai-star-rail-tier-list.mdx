## Tier List Criteria

This tier list is designed for new accounts. It values characters that work in many teams, perform well before perfect relics, and help players clear story and farming content consistently.

The ranking criteria are:

- Account flexibility
- Low-investment performance
- Story and farming speed
- Survival support
- Long-term team value

## S Tier

S tier characters are strong across many modes and make early account progression smoother.

| Role | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Premium sustain | Keeps weak relic teams alive |
| Universal support | Fits many damage dealers |
| Efficient damage dealer | Clears waves and bosses quickly |

## A Tier

A tier characters are excellent but may need specific teams, better relics, or more careful resource planning. They are still worth building if they fill a role your account lacks.

## B Tier

B tier characters can clear content, especially early story chapters, but should not receive all limited resources unless you enjoy their playstyle or need their element.

## New Account Team Template

Use this simple team shell:

1. One main damage dealer
2. One sustain unit
3. One support or debuffer
4. One flexible breaker or sub damage dealer

## Pull Priority Advice

Prioritize characters that solve account-wide problems. A great sustain or universal support often does more for a new account than a narrow damage upgrade.
