## Quick Answer

Elden Ring has several endings, but most players should think of them as final-choice unlocks rather than separate full routes. The default ending is available naturally, while Age of Stars requires Ranni's questline and Frenzied Flame requires a specific underground commitment that can override other endings unless reversed. Other Elden Lord variants depend on major NPC questlines. If you want maximum flexibility, finish questlines before the final boss, avoid accepting the Frenzied Flame unless you truly want it, and keep backup notes before major late-game decisions.

## Introduction

Endings in Elden Ring are easy to misunderstand because the game lets you unlock multiple outcomes and choose near the end. The danger is not missing every ending; it is accidentally committing to one that overrides the others. This guide explains the main ending families, what unlocks them, and how to plan a playthrough without spoiling the final decision too early.

## Detailed Guide

### Main Ending Families

The default Elden Lord ending is the baseline route and requires no elaborate side quest. Several variants modify that result through NPC questlines and mending runes. Age of Stars comes from Ranni's questline and is chosen through her summon sign after the final boss. Lord of Frenzied Flame is the most dangerous for flexible players because accepting the flame can force that ending unless you complete the reversal path. Treat each ending as a flag you unlock through quests, then choose at the end.

### How to Keep Options Open

If you want to see as many endings as possible, progress major NPC questlines before burning through late-game milestones. Ranni, Fia, Dung Eater, Goldmask, and Frenzied Flame routes all have important steps that can be missed or delayed. The safest plan is to finish exploration and quest cleanup before triggering the final stretch. Avoid opening the Frenzied Flame door unless you are ready for that commitment. If you do accept it, learn the reversal process before defeating the final boss.

### Questlines That Matter

Ranni's quest unlocks Age of Stars and sends you through Nokron, Ainsel River, Lake of Rot, and Moonlight Altar. Fia's route connects to Deeproot Depths and a mending rune. Goldmask and Brother Corhyn require careful tracking across Altus and Leyndell. Dung Eater's path is darker and involves Seedbed Curses. These routes are valuable for more than endings: they reveal hidden areas, bosses, spells, items, and lore.

### Choosing the Ending

After the final boss, interact only with the option that matches the ending you want. Ranni's sign is separate from the main interaction, which is why players sometimes miss it after completing her quest. If multiple mending runes are available, the game lets you choose among them. Read the prompt carefully and do not rush through the final arena.

## Tips

- Avoid Frenzied Flame if you want ending flexibility.
- Complete NPC questlines before the final boss.
- Read the final prompt carefully before choosing.
- Use separate notes for quest state if chasing multiple endings.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming Ranni's ending triggers automatically.
- Accepting Frenzied Flame without understanding the lock-in.
- Ignoring NPC questlines until after major world changes.
- Rushing the final interaction and choosing the wrong ending.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many Elden Ring endings are there?

There are several ending outcomes, including the default Elden Lord route, multiple mending rune variants, Age of Stars, and Lord of Frenzied Flame.

### Can I unlock multiple endings in one playthrough?

You can unlock multiple ending options, but normally choose one ending at the end of the playthrough.

### Does Ranni's quest force her ending?

No. It unlocks the option, but you still need to choose her summon sign after the final boss.

### Can Frenzied Flame be reversed?

Yes, but it requires a specific late-game reversal route. It is better to avoid accepting it unless you want that ending.

### Which ending should beginners choose?

Beginners should keep options open, finish the quests they enjoy, and choose the ending that matches their preferred story outcome.
